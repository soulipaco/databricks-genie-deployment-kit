# 09 — Benchmark Question Bank

Standard question patterns by category. Use as inspiration for generating benchmarks.
Replace `{{ENTITY}}`, `{{KPI}}`, `{{PERIOD}}`, and `{{DIMENSION}}` with room-specific values.

## Category A: Summary / Ranking
- "What is the {{KPI}} by {{DIMENSION}} for {{PERIOD}}?"
- "How does {{KPI}} break down by {{DIMENSION}}?"
- "Show me {{KPI}} for all {{ENTITY}} in the last month"
- "Which {{DIMENSION}} has the highest {{KPI}}?"
- "Compare {{KPI}} across all {{DIMENSION}} values"

## Category B: Trend Analysis
- "How has {{KPI}} trended over the last 12 weeks?"
- "Show monthly {{KPI}} trend for the last 6 months"
- "What is the weekly {{KPI}} trend year to date?"
- "How has {{KPI}} changed month over month?"

## Category C: Top-N Ranking
- "Who are the top 5 {{ENTITY}} by {{KPI}}?"
- "What are the 10 highest performing {{ENTITY}}?"
- "Bottom 5 {{ENTITY}} by {{KPI}} last month"
- "Best {{ENTITY}} in {{DIMENSION}} for {{PERIOD}}"

## Category D: Comparison
- "How does {{ENTITY_A}} compare to {{ENTITY_B}} on {{KPI}}?"
- "Compare last month {{KPI}} vs the previous month"
- "How does {{DIMENSION_VALUE}} compare to the overall average?"
- "Period over period {{KPI}} change for {{ENTITY}}"

## Category E: Pareto / Root-Cause
- "What are the top reasons for {{ISSUE}}?"
- "Show a Pareto of {{CATEGORY}} driving {{OUTCOME}}"
- "Which {{CATEGORY}} accounts for most of the {{METRIC}}?"

## Category F: Entity Lookup
- "What is {{ENTITY}}'s {{KPI}} for {{PERIOD}}?"
- "Show all KPIs for {{ENTITY}} in the last month"
- "How did {{ENTITY}} perform vs target?"

## Category G: Parameterized
- "What was {{KPI}} between [date range]?"
- "Show {{ENTITY}} performance for [specific period]"

## Category H: Complex
- "What would the {{KPI}} be without {{ENTITY}}?"
- "Which {{ENTITY}} changed {{KPI}} the most this month vs last?"
- "Distribution of {{ENTITY}} by {{KPI}} percentile"
